✿ Our Story

A Home Filled with Warmth & Blossoms

Nestled in a quiet neighbourhood, Lily Homestay was born from a simple dream — to offer travellers more than just a room. We wanted to share a home where you feel welcomed, rested, and truly cared for.

Our property is lovingly decorated with fresh flowers year-round, creating an atmosphere of calm and natural beauty. Every morning begins with birdsong from our garden and ends with the gentle scent of blossoms drifting through your window.

We take pride in personal, attentive hosting — whether you need local tips, a home-cooked breakfast, or simply a quiet corner to unwind, we're here for you.
